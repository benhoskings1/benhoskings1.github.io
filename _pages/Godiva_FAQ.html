Coventry Godiva Harriers App FAQ

Is the app optional?

Yes. Your participation is entirely optional.

How do I remove my consent for my data to be used?

Please contact Ben to express your wish to remove your data from the system. Your data will be removed within 1 working day of your expression to withdraw your data.
